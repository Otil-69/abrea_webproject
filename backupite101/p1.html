BOOKS & INTERVIEWS

SUNRISE ON THE REAPING

Dystopian | 2023 | Scholastic Press

As the day dawns on the fiftieth annual Hunger Games, fear grips the districts of Panem. This year, in honor of the Quarter Quell, twice as many tributes will be taken from their homes. Back in District 12, Haymitch Abernathy is trying not to think too hard about his chances. All he cares about is making it through the day and being with the girl he loves.

THE BALLAD OF SONGBIRDS AND SNAKES

Dystopian | 2020 | Scholastic Press

It is the morning of the reaping that will kick off the tenth annual Hunger Games. In the Capitol, eighteen-year-old Coriolanus Snow is preparing for his one shot at glory as a mentor in the Games. The once-mighty house of Snow has fallen on hard times, its fate hanging on the slender chance that Coriolanus will be able to outcharm, outwit, and outmaneuver his fellow students to mentor the winning tribute.

YEAR OF THE JUNGLE

Memoir | 2013 | Scholastic Press

When Suzy's father is called to war in a faraway jungle, she struggles to deal with his absence.

MOCKINGJAY

Dystopian | 2010 | Scholastic Press

Katniss Everdeen, girl on fire, has survived, even though her home has been destroyed. There are rebels. There are new leaders. A revolution is unfolding.

CATCHING FIRE

Dystopian | 2009 | Scholastic Press

Much to her shock, Katniss has fueled an unrest she's afraid she cannot stop. As time draws near for Katniss and Peeta to visit the districts on the Capitol's cruel Victory Tour, the stakes are higher than ever. If they can't prove their love convincingly, the consequences will be horrifying.

THE HUNGER GAMES

Dystopian | 2008 | Scholastic Press

Katniss is a 16-year-old girl living with her mother and younger sister in the poorest district of Panem. When her sister is chosen by lottery, Katniss steps up to go in her place in the deadly Hunger Games.
