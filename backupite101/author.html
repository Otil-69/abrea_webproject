Suzanne Collins
Author · Screenwriter

Suzanne Collins is a highly acclaimed American author and screenwriter best known for creating The Hunger Games series. Her works highlight survival, courage, and society's influence.

Career & Achievements: Started in children’s TV, wrote Little Bear, Oswald, then transitioned to bestselling books. The Hunger Games series sold over 100 million copies and inspired blockbuster films.
Born: August 10, 1962
